Bookit - A case-study for booking train tickets

Introduction

Bookit is a conceptual mobile and web application idea, designed to improve the current train booking system in Nigeria, as more train routes are introduced.

Motivation

I have for a long time loved trains and since the revamping of the current train system in Nigeria, I have taken a train no less than Thirty times in the past three years, The current system has a lot of flaws from arrival of the passenger at the train station to departure from the train station to the departure of the train from the train station as well as the arrival at the passengers destination.

Aim

The aim of this case study is to proffer solutions as a product designer to help increase customer satisfaction levels by designing a mobile app, that can help solve most of the pain-points experienced by passengers from the process of purchasing a ticket to the end of their trip.

Understanding the User

Since there was no readily available way of gathering data. I had to rely on past experiences, discussions i have had with other passengers and blog and blogs stories to help identify and understand the pain-points and design solutions.

My design process helped to give me a lot insight into the various users and user patterns, which helped in creating user personas, scenarios and stories for the various users as illustrated below.

Problems and Pain-Points

As the popularity of the train increased demand increased, in a study carried out in 2018 the average amount passengers serviced per day was 5,000 (Link to study) . As at the time of writing this the number must have doubled due to factors such as the soaring popularity of the of the train compared to road transport and safety the issue of safety. Hence producing a new flow.
Due the demand exceeding supply certain avoidable pain points were created causing a lot of backlog on the system such that it became extremely difficult for a passenger without influence to purchase a ticket. Based on observation and my experience as well as other passengers experience the following were the popular passenger complaints were generated in no particular order.

  • Absence of any form of online booking
  • Lack of customer support
  • Ticket Racketeering
  • Biased rendering of services
  • Confusing departure schedules
  • Pricing structure
  • Overcrowding at ticketing station and long queues

Solutions

There is no current booking system to help eliminate some of the pain-points experienced by passengers, by designing a booking system on web and mobile (App and Progressive web App) equipped with a identity verification API and payment system to keep up with demand and increase revenue generated.

Design Guide

By creating a user personas, user stories and understanding their preferences, i was able to design with data that helped me in understanding the user's color mood, basic interaction style and how to get the user to complete a basic task easily.
